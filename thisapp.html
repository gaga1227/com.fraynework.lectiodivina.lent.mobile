About This App

The Catholic Diocese of Broken Bay has been creating lectio divina guides for the Seasons of Lent and Advent since 2009. During the past 4 years, these guides have reached over 70,000 people across every diocese in Australia and beyond.

More information about the reflective prayer of lectio divina can be found on the Diocesan website www.dbb.org.au. An electronic booklet and reflection pages are also available for download.

If you would like to know more about this App or have feedback you’d like to send us please contact us at lectio@dbb.org.au.
